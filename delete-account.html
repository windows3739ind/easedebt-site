EaseDebt Account Deletion

EaseDebt allows users to permanently delete their account and associated data directly from within the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open EaseDebt.
  2. Go to Me.
  3. Open Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your password.
  6. Confirm account deletion.

What is deleted?

  • Your EaseDebt account.
  • Your profile.
  • Your debts and commitments.
  • Your payments.
  • Your reminders.
  • Your settings and preferences.
  • All associated cloud data.

Data retention

After successful deletion, your account and associated personal data are permanently deleted from EaseDebt's systems unless retention is required by applicable law.
